Children's Garden

A very extensive, diverse, and beautiful flower garden. It is generally rather unoccuppied and provides many things to see and do. It makes a great morning or afternoon adventure.

Beaumont Tower/Park

Making up the center portion of east circle, Beaumont Tower and it's accompanying park provide the ideal place to take a walk, see the leaves, or just sprawl out on the ground.

Broad Museum

An interesting and free modern art museum. You never know what you're going to get.
