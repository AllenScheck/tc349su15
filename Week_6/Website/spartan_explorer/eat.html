Eat

Brody Square

The biggest cafeteria on campus, one of the largest in the world, Brody has a very extensive menu, great variety, and is a great place to go eat.

MSU Union

A small and unassuming lounge with comfortable seating, delicious pizza, and the occasional live show.

Crossroads Food Court

Busy, but with good reason. This food court located in the international center has the comfort foods we all often desire.
